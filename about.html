About

Building brands that resonate.

I'm Callum Dunne — a brand and creative strategist based in Dublin, Ireland. Over seven years I've worked across FMCG, automotive, QSR, travel, and hospitality, figuring out what makes a brand worth paying attention to.

I don't buy the idea that brand strategy and creative are two different jobs. The work I find most interesting sits where brand, culture, and creative strategy meet — where a qualitative and quantitative insight becomes work that doesn't need explaining.
